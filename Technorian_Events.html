TOWER MAKING

Rules:

1. You may use only the materials supplied. You may break the Popsicle sticks if so desired, but no replacements will be provided and no cutting devices will be allowed.
2. The base of tower cannot be glued to the table to flow or any other surface or object.
3. Your tower may not touch anything except the base that you build it on.
4. You are allowed to use as many of the glue you are given, but no more.
5. You can use up to 90 craft sticks, but you do not need to use them all.
6. The minimum height of a tower is approximately 40 cm. Width of base of the tower should be 10 cm, Middle portion of the tower should be 6 cm and the top portion of the tower should be 2 cm.
7. You will have exactly 2:00 hours to build your tower. (Hint: get started right away; don’t waste too much time planning.)
8. No coaching will be allowed.
