Fair Games
Fairs bring a sense of joy and excitement to everyone. So come one and come all to enjoy these fun Fair games!!

Rules:

DAY 1:
1. Musical Chairs:
• At the start of music players have to walk clockwise around the chairs.
• When the music stops, players have to sit on the nearest empty chair quickly.
• One person will be left standing without a chair, and they will be out of the game.
• And this goes on until two persons are left with one chair, the one who sits when the music stops are the winner.
2. Card Tower:
• Players will be provided with a deck of cards.
• They have to build a 4-level tower with the cards in 5 min.
• The one who builds within a given time is the winner.
3. Water Pong:
• 10 cups will be arranged in a form of triangle half filled with water.
• Players will be provided with a table tennis ball.
• They have to throw the ball in cups in one bounce.
• No ball should directly land in the cup without bouncing.
• The player who puts the balls in all the cups will be the winner.
4. Tissue Paper Game:
• A tissue will be spread on the table, with a cup of water at the end.
• Player has to roll the tissue without spilling the water or breaking the tissue.
• The one who rolls the tissue from end to the other without spilling or breaking wins.
DAY 2:
1. Slow Cycle:
• Players will be provided with cycles 16
• They have to ride the cycle as slow as possible.
• Players should not touch their feet on the ground.
• The last one to reach the finish line will be the winner.
2. Toss the Hoop:
• Players will be given 5 hoops / rings.
• Players have to throw the rings at the stake attached to the cardboard.
• The player who hooks more rings will win.
3. Balloon Blast:
• Players will be provided with balloons.
• They have to blow the balloon at a certain point and then blast it by sitting on it.
• The player who blows and blasts more balloons in a given time will be the winner.
4. Blow and Lift:
• Players will be provided with balloons and cups.
• They have to blow the balloon and lift the cup with it.
• Using the balloons, they have to build a tower of 3 levels within given time
