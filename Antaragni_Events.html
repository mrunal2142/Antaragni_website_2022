Box Cricket

About Event

Box cricket is a mini version of our beloved cricket. It’s as much fun playing cricket in a box as in a stadium. So show your sportsmanship and team spirit to win the best game with the best possible score and unexpected wins!

Rules:

1. Each team shall include 6 players.
2. There will be 3 overs per inning one bowler can bowl a maximum of 1 over.
3. Only underarm bowling is allowed.
4. The team must report a venue 15 min before the given time.
5. One extra run will be given for No ball, dead ball, and wide ball.
6. 4th NO ball in the same over results in bowler disqualification and extra 2 penalty runs.
7. Only four are allowed. Hitting six will be given out.
8. One tip one hand catch will be declared out.
9. In the case of Draw, the maximum no. of boundaries will be considered.
10. Umpire decision will be the final decision. No arguments will be tolerated against the umpire.


Awards:


1st Prize - 2000/-
2nd Prize - 1500/-


Entry Fee:

Group - 200/-

Rangpeeth (Nukkad Natak)

About Event

Street play is one of the oldest theatre forms where the artists enact in public places with no specific target audience. It is a genre of drama performed with music and dance which is known for its simplicity and creative execution. It serves as a metaphor that captures an image of moral nature which every layman can relate to. A big shoutout to all theatre fanatics with a passion for drama to release the actor within, for Antaragni '22, presents Rangpeeth- a colossal display of theatrics in the form of street plays.

Rules:

1. Offensive Language is not allowed in the skit.
2. Religious sentiments should not be hurt.
3. Open-minded and awareness topics are appreciated.
4. Play shouldn't exceed the time limit of 10mins.
5. Coordinators have the right to change any rule at any point in time.
6. Time Limit - 3 Min
